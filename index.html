Arrow: The Final Season

The eighth and final season of Arrow brings the long-running DC superhero show to a gripping conclusion: Oliver Queen, the Green Arrow, faces his greatest challenge yet as he prepares for the inevitable multiverse-crisis. With the fate of the world at stake, Oliver is joined by his trusted team and familiar faces from across the Arrowverse as they fight to protect Star City one last time. The season stars Stephen Amell (Heels) as Oliver Queen/Green Arrow, David Ramsey (John Diggle), Emily Bett Rickards (The Flash) as Felicity Smoak, and Katie Cassidy (The Flash) as Laurel Lance. The final season is directed by James Bamford (Arrow) and Beth Schwartz (Legends of Tomorrow), with Marc Guggenheim (Crisis on Infinite Earths) and Greg Berlanti (The Flash) serving as executive producers.
